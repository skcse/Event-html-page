STRUCTURA

AIM::

Teams have to build a tall structure and a cantilever platform (like Inverted L-shaped) with material provided during the events. The structure will be used for simulating bungee jumping with predetermined weight.


RULES::

    We have to make tall structure of minimum height (H) 30cm and a cantilever platform of minimum length(L) 10cm.
    From the last portion of cantilever platform we will stimulate bungee jump with certain weight (W)
    Final score score will be calculated by the given formulae :: 10*H + 10*L + W

PRIZE::

1st Prize -- INR 5000
2nd Prize -- INR 3000
3rd Prize -- INR 2000
